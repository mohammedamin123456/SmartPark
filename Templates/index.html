About Us

Smart Park is an intuitive application designed exclusively for the Cal State Fullerton campus. Our goal is to simplify parking on campus, saving time and reducing stress for students, faculty, and visitors.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Parking Availability: Get live updates on available parking spots across campus.
  • Occupancy Stats: View parking lot occupancy percentages for better decision-making.
  • Interactive Map: Navigate easily to available parking lots using our user-friendly map.
  • Reservations (Coming Soon): Secure your parking spot during busy hours.

Our mission is to enhance the campus experience by providing a smarter way to park. Let Smart Park make your day at CSUF more efficient and stress-free!
